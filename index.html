Jack Luck Games

We are a few passionate gamers and software developers. We are deeply enthusiastic about creating and developing software applications that inspire and engage our users. We are constantly learning and experimenting with new technologies and approaches, and we love to discover game play ideas that just make you keep coming back for more.

Our current focus is developing 2D games for mobile, and we are hoping to release our first game in March 2024 on the Apple App Store. We will follow-up with a Android release shortly after the iOS release. The first game is called Rocket Ripper. You can read more about this first game below.

We are starting out as a family business with one full-time developer (dad) and two sons who could be considered full-time gamers when their schoolwork is done. The sons have been instrumental in ideas and testing for this first game release. We hope to start a youtube channel soon and begin some tutorials on Unity development. Like many indie game developers, we have found Google searching and YouTube videos to be instrumental in helping us solve the problem of the day. We'd like to take some time and give back as well. Many thanks go to Unity for providing a great free-tier development environment as well!


Rocket Ripper

Coming to the Apple App Store Soon

(Planned Release in March 2024)

Embark on the ultimate space adventure as the fearless pilot of your very own drone rocket ship! Brace yourself for an adrenaline-fueled journey through the cosmos, where danger lurks around every corner. Blast through the void, dodging treacherous asteroids and engaging in heart-pounding dogfights with enemy ships hell-bent on your destruction.

With stunning graphics and edge-of-your-seat gameplay, you'll feel the thrill of space exploration like never before. Upgrade your ship and unlock special abilities to unleash devastating attacks on your foes. But be warned: the challenges ahead are as vast as the universe itself.

Do you have what it takes to navigate the stars and emerge victorious? Join the cosmic battle now and become the ultimate space hero in this thrilling mobile game! Try out the "How To Play" tutorial after downloading for some tips. The upper left corner menu offers Ad Removal, Rocket Ship Store, and numerous settings.
